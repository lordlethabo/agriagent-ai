Reasoning architecture

Specialist agents working as one decision system.

AgriAgent combines validation, seasonal reasoning, safety screening, planning, water intelligence, risk analysis, and global impact scoring.

Season & Location Agent

Detects regional context and seasonal planning constraints from the farmer's location.

Safety Agent

Flags risky financial, chemical, water, legal, and disease-related recommendations.

Planner Agent

Creates farm layout, production strategy, crop planning, and scale-up roadmap.

Water Agent

Recommends irrigation, storage, soil moisture protection, and drought resilience actions.

Risk Agent

Identifies climate, pest, financial, operational, and market risks with mitigation actions.

Global Impact Agent

Scores food security, climate resilience, water sustainability, and economic impact.

Coordinator Agent

Combines specialist outputs into a final decision-ready advisory recommendation.

Report Generator

Converts the advisory output into a downloadable PDF report for farmers and stakeholders.

Agent workflow

From farmer input to verified advisory output.

The workflow is designed to make the system more reliable than a basic chatbot by separating reasoning into specialist steps.

01 Farmer Input

Collects location, farming goal, farm size, and main challenge.

02 Validation

Checks whether the farm profile is specific enough for analysis.

03 Season Context

Infers hemisphere, season, and climate planning context.

04 Safety

Detects risky chemical, water, legal, financial, or disease terms.

05 Planning

Creates practical farm strategy and scale-up roadmap.

06 Water

Recommends irrigation, water storage, and conservation actions.

07 Risk

Assesses climate, pest, market, operational, and financial risks.

08 Impact

Scores food security, climate, water, and economic outcomes.
